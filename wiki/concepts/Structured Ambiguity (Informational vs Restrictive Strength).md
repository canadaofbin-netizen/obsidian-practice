# Structured Ambiguity
**Core Idea:** Environments must be weak in restrictive constraints to allow autonomy, but strong in informational clarity to prevent cognitive collapse.

[[Paper - Judge 2015]] champions the "weak situation" (low constraints) as the optimal ground for Trait Activation. However, [[Paper - Calderwood 2023]] mathematically bifurcates strength, proving that removing constraints without preserving *Informational Strength* (clarity and consistency) plunges the worker into an entropic void. Absolute autonomy without clarity induces massive cognitive strain and emotional exhaustion. 
The synthesis is **Structured Ambiguity**: an architectural sweet spot that remains deliberately weak in *Restrictive Strength* to unleash peak performance, while highly elevated in *Informational Strength* to provide cognitive scaffolding against burnout.
